RCTW-17

ICDAR2017 Competition on Reading Chinese Text in the Wild

Register Now

RCTW-17 is a competition on reading Chinese Text in images. For training and testing, we provide a large-scale dataset that consists of various kinds of images, including street views, posters, menus, indoor scenes, and screenshots. See Dataset and Task & Evaluation for details.

Timetable

  • Registration open: January 20 - March 31, 2017
  • Test dataset available: March 15, 2017
  • Submission open: March 15, 2017
  • Submission deadline: March 31, 2017
